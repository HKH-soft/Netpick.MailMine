# User Personas - Netpick for Iranian Market

## Persona 1: سارا (Sara) - Startup Founder
**Age:** 28 | **Location:** Tehran | **Role:** Tech Startup CEO

### Background
- Runs a 15-person SaaS startup in Tehran Digital Innovation District
- Needs to track investor leads, manage team tasks, and handle client invoices
- Uses Instagram and LinkedIn for lead generation

### Goals
- Track investor conversations and follow-ups
- Manage product roadmap with her team
- Generate invoices for international clients (USD/EUR)
- Store contracts and pitch decks securely

### Pain Points
- No integrated system for lead tracking
- Manual invoice creation in Excel
- Team uses WhatsApp for task coordination (chaotic)
- Documents scattered across Google Drive and email

### Netpick Value
- DealFarm: Track investor pipeline with stages
- TaskFarm: Kanban board for product roadmap
- FinanceFarm: Multi-currency invoices
- FileFarm: Centralized document storage
